About Us

Our bakery

Just Cupcakes is a well established bakery located in Dublin city centre. We specialize in high quality cupcakes, made fresh daily, and we offer a wide variety of flavours and designs. We bake our products from scratch and we pride ourselves in using only the finest and preservative-free ingredients. All our cupcakes are made using Irish butter, free range eggs, natural food colourings and of course, baked in a professional environment.

We started as a small farmer's market stall in 2005 to cater for the growing demand in the market for quality cupcakes. Our store has grown steadily since then with the addition of multiple markets in the Dublin area and through our website sales and online ordering facility. Now we deliver everywhere in Ireland for as little as 10 euro.

Have fun browsing our website and if there's something special that you would like, but don't see, let us know.
